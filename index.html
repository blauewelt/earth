earth

open climate data on a globe

Daily NASA GIBS imagery — most layers update within hours; a few lag by days or have ended (they render empty past their range).

What is this?

earth is a prototype for exploring the world's open climate data on a 3D globe — and, eventually, for generating predictions from it (e.g., will the AMOC collapse, and when?).

It renders time-dynamic NASA GIBS imagery with zero API keys, marks the AMOC monitoring arrays and reference GHG stations, and ships a machine-readable catalog of 241 open climate datasets.

Roadmap

  • Zarr-streamed gridded fields (ERA5, CMIP6) rendered client-side
  • Live point layers: Climate TRACE facilities, Argo floats, OpenAQ
  • AMOC dashboard: RAPID/OSNAP transports + early-warning indicators
  • Prediction pipeline: statistical tipping-time estimation (Ditlevsen 2023, van Westen 2024)
